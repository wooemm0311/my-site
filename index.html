Welcome to my tattoo and body piercing shop!

Here at Black Lotus, we do tattoos from all genres: Japanese, tribal, nature, and different cultures. We can do Celtic crosses or knots, the Koi fish, or Skyline or Traditional tattoos.


Traditional tattoos are “old school” tattoos, such as anchors, pinup girls, words and banners. The Celtic genre includes Welsh, Gaelic, and Breton imagery; this style usually represents the heritage of these places. Tribal tattoos usually have symmetrical design and bold, geometric shapes in black ink.


In addition to tattoos, we provide piercing services for lip, tongue, ear, nose, and stomach .
